Process

How every visa case flows through Vizezz

A structured five-stage workflow that gives you visibility and control at every checkpoint.

01

Request Logged

Case initiated via platform or account manager. Employee details and documents captured in structured intake.

02

Document Validation

All required documents verified against authority checklists. Missing items flagged with automated notifications.

03

Government Workflow

Submission to MOHRE, ICP, and relevant authorities. PRO coordination and escalation managed by our operations team.

04

Status Tracking

Live status updates visible on the platform dashboard. HR receives proactive alerts at each stage transition.

05

Closure & Reporting

Case closed with document delivery, MIS updated, and governance reports generated for HR and leadership review.
